My Parents

Claudio Parra Senior

He is hardest worker I know. My father always keeps a great sense of humor even in the darkest moments. His capacity to show love to others through service, many times even with a high cost to himself, is something that now characterizes our family thanks to his great example.

Maria Inostroza

She has devoted her life to raising every single one of us. After the diagnosis of two of my brothers with PKU (allergic to protein!). She strived for about a decade to find something tastier to feed her children. My mom ended up creating her own flour opening a whole line of new products for my brothers!
